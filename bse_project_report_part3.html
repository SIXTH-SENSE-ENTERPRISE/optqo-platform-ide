Key Functionalities

\n\n

1. Data Deduplication and Standardization

\n

\n A significant portion of the codebase is dedicated to data standardization and deduplication. This includes:\n

\n
    \n
  • Name Standardization - Using SAS Data Quality functions to standardize individual and organization names
    • \n
  • Address Standardization - Processing address fields to create consistent formats
    • \n
  • PAN Number Validation - Validating and standardizing PAN (Permanent Account Number) fields
    • \n
  • Match Code Generation - Creating match codes for names and addresses at different confidence levels (75%, 85%)
    • \n
  • Cluster ID Assignment - Grouping similar records based on match codes
    • \n
\n\n

2. Insider Trading Detection

\n

\n The system implements sophisticated algorithms to detect potential insider trading patterns around company announcements:\n

\n
    \n
  • Announcement Analysis - Scanning company announcements for significant events like mergers, dividends, rights issues
    • \n
  • Trading Pattern Analysis - Analyzing buying and selling patterns before and after announcements
    • \n
  • Statistical Modeling - Using scoring models to identify suspicious trading activity
    • \n
  • Transaction Profiling - Examining transaction sizes, timing, and price variations
    • \n
  • Profit Calculation - Calculating actual and notional profits from potentially suspicious trades
    • \n
\n

\n The insider trading detection algorithm pays special attention to:\n

\n
    \n
  • Trading volume relative to market volume
    • \n
  • Timing of trades relative to announcements
    • \n
  • Holding patterns (long vs. short positions)
    • \n
  • Buy/sell ratios before and after announcements
    • \n
  • Profit realization patterns
    • \n
\n\n

3. Reporting and Output Generation

\n

\n The system generates various reports to support analysis:\n

\n
    \n
  • Announcement Reports - Details of company announcements with classification
    • \n
  • Summary Reports - Suspicious trading activity summaries
    • \n
  • Transaction Reports - Detailed transaction logs for suspicious accounts
    • \n
  • Market Behavior Reports - Analysis of trading patterns across the market
    • \n
\n

\n Reports are exported to Excel format with multiple sheets for different views of the data.\n
